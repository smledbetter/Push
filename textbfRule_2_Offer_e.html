Rule 2: Offer empathetic rationales.
An empathetic rationale explains to the user why they should do a thing, given their goals. It explains why any request from the app is personally relevant to them.
Example: “Hey [Name], last time we spoke you mentioned you wanted to track your body weight, this week. Here’s your friendly reminder!”
Limits: A limit of this technique is that you have to do the hard work of helping users figure out what they actually want. They might not know what they want, or they might change their mind. But it still on you as the app designer to facilitate that process and to check in regularly to make sure they still want it.
